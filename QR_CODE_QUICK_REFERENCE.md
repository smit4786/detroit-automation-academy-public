## ‚úÖ If QR Code Isn't Scanning

1. **Check lighting** - Move to brighter area
2. **Clean camera lens** - Wipe with soft cloth
3. **Move closer/farther** - Try 6-8 inches away
4. **Try different app** - Use Google Lens or dedicated QR scanner
5. **Show them manually:** Students can type the URL or search "DAA enrollment"
