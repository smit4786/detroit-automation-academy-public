# Module 2: Apple Safety Features

**Objective:** Master the built-in tools that help maintain a healthy and safe relationship with technology.

## Key Concepts
- **Boundary Setting:** Using Screen Time to balance productivity and rest.
- **Family Sharing Architecture:** How parents and youth can coordinate on app purchases and safety.
- **Content & Privacy Restrictions:** Customizing your device to block inappropriate content autonomously.

## Technical Skills
- Configuring 'Communication Safety' to detect sensitive media.
- Setting up 'Downtime' and 'App Limits' for deep work blocks.
