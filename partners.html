Use of Funds

Your support directly enables:

  • Equipment: Raspberry Pi kits ($12,600), 3D printers, laser cutters
  • Staff: Lead instructors, teaching assistants, curriculum developers
  • Infrastructure: JupyterHub, GitHub Actions, hosting and support
  • Materials: Sensors, motors, fabrication materials, snacks/meals
  • Scholarships: Ensure access for low-income students

📊 Investment ROI

Direct ROI

  • Talent Pipeline: Hire trained, vetted candidates for internships/entry-level roles
  • Industry Alignment: Curriculum reflects manufacturing 4.0 trends
  • Market Differentiation: Show commitment to Detroit workforce development

Community Impact

  • Equity: 60%+ of students from underrepresented backgrounds
  • Economic Mobility: Median salary for automation technicians: $55K+/year
  • Detroit Pride: Built by Detroiters, for Detroiters—demonstrable community reinvestment
