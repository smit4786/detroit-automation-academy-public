Four Progressive Phases

From first circuit to fully autonomous robot — in 12 weeks.

PHASE 1 · GPIO & HARDWARE

🔌 Electronics Control

Learn Raspberry Pi GPIO programming, control LEDs and buttons, understand hardware fundamentals, and build event-driven Python applications with real peripherals.

PHASE 2 · CAD & FABRICATION

🏗️ 3D Design & Laser Cutting

Design parametric 3D models, generate STL files for 3D printing, create G-code for laser cutting, and fabricate custom parts for your robotics project.

PHASE 3 · AUTONOMOUS SYSTEMS

🤖 Rover Navigation & Autonomy

Program autonomous rover behavior, implement navigation algorithms, test in simulated environments, and deploy your code on real hardware at the final showcase.

PHASE 4 · ONLINE SAFETY & APPLE

🛡️ Digital Citizenship

Master online safety, privacy sovereignty, and secure artifact creation within the Apple ecosystem to responsibly navigate the internet.
