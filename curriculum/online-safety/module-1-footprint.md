# Module 1: Digital Footprint & Reputation

**Objective:** Understand that every action online leaves a trace and learn how to manage your digital legacy.

## Key Concepts
- **The Permanent Record:** Everything you post, search, or share contributes to your digital footprint.
- **For-Profit Mindset:** In the DAA ecosystem, we treat our digital identity as a professional asset.
- **Privacy as a Choice:** Learning what to share publicly and what to keep private.

## Technical Skills
- Auditing your 'Shared with You' content on iOS.
- Managing 'Sign in with Apple' to limit data exposure to third-party apps.
