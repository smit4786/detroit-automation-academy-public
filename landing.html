Three Progressive Phases

PHASE 1: GPIO & HARDWARE

🔌 Electronics Control

Learn Raspberry Pi GPIO programming, control LEDs and buttons, understand hardware fundamentals, and build event-driven applications.

PHASE 2: CAD & FABRICATION

🏗️ 3D Design & Laser Cutting

Design parametric 3D models, generate STL files for 3D printing, create G-code for laser cutting, and fabricate real parts.

PHASE 3: AUTONOMOUS SYSTEMS

🤖 Rover Navigation & Autonomy

Program autonomous rover behavior, implement navigation algorithms, test in simulated environments, and deploy on real hardware.
