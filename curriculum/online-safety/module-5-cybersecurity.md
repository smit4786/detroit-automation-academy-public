# Module 5: Cybersecurity Basics & Passkeys

**Objective:** Move beyond passwords to the next generation of account security.

## Key Concepts
- **The Death of the Password:** Why traditional passwords are weak and how **Passkeys** replace them.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Adding a second layer of defense to your Apple Account.
- **Secure Downloads:** Only using the App Store and verified sources for software.

## Technical Skills
- Creating and using a Passkey on iOS/macOS.
- Auditing 'Saved Passwords' and identifying compromised credentials.
