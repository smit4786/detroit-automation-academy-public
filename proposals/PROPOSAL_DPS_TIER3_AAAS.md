## 1. Executive Summary
The Detroit Automation Academy (DAA) proposes a Tier 3 partnership with Detroit Public Schools to scale high-fidelity automation and robotics education across the district. By licensing "The Engine," DPS gains full access to the DAA curriculum, the Oculus Agent Fleet for instructors, and a native mobile student portal.

## 2. Key Deliverables
- **District-Wide Curriculum License:** Unlimited student seats for DAA Phases 1, 2, and 3.
- **Instructor Fleet Access:** Provisioning of specialized AI agents (Codebase-Auditor, Wealth-Analyst, etc.) to assist DPS instructors in technical delivery.
- **Oculus Mobile Portal:** Native iOS/Android application for student enrollment, telemetry, and project tracking.
- **Onboarding & Lab Setup:** 40 hours of on-site setup and instructor training ($10,000 implementation fee).
