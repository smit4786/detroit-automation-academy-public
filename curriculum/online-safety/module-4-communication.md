# Module 4: Secure Communication

**Objective:** Learn to use communication tools safely while identifying and mitigating social risks.

## Key Concepts
- **Identifying Phishing:** Recognizing 'Social Engineering' in iMessage, Mail, and gaming platforms.
- **Reporting & Blocking:** The ethics and mechanics of removing toxic or dangerous contacts.
- **Safety Check:** How to quickly audit who has access to your location and information.

## Technical Skills
- Using 'Hide My Email' to prevent spam and tracking.
- Performing an iOS 'Safety Check' to manage shared access.
