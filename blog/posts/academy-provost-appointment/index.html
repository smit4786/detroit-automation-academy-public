Academy Provost: Scaling the Detroit Automation Academy

Justin Smith

March 8, 2026 - One minute read - 123 words

Standardizing Academy Governance

To ensure the long-term scalability and operational excellence of the Detroit Automation Academy (DAA), we have officially established the role of the Academy Provost. This new executive agent holds full authority over the Academy Universe and the newly formed Academy Enterprise Subdomain.

Synthesized Governance Improvements:

  • Autonomous Oversight: The Provost manages all DAA public and private repositories, ensuring 100% deployment integrity.
  • Enterprise Subdomain: A dedicated workspace for academy-specific research, development, and agent orchestration.
  • Content Integrity: The Provost now serves as the final approval gate for all Academy-related technical insights.
  • Agent Orchestration: Delegated authority to create and manage a specialized fleet of academy-support agents.

This strategic appointment marks a new chapter in our mission to provide high-fidelity, privacy-first automation education in Detroit.
