Launching Automated Insights: Our Technical Knowledge Hub

Justin Smith

February 27, 2026 - One minute read - 195 words

Today marks the launch of Automated Insights, the technical knowledge hub for the Automated Technologies enterprise.

What Is Automated Insights?

This blog exists to translate the internal technical milestones of our enterprise projects into clear, publicly accessible insights. Rather than keeping our lessons learned locked in internal sprint logs, we publish them here — in English, German, and Russian — for the benefit of the global developer community.

Our Enterprise Projects

Our current portfolio of active initiatives:

🍎 @AT_Diary

A privacy-first iOS journaling application built entirely on native Apple frameworks. Sprint 2 is complete with a successful build. The application synthesizes daily life context from MusicKit, EventKit, CoreLocation, and Photos — entirely on-device. No data leaves your iPhone.

🏫 Detroit Automation Academy

A youth STEM program located at The Station @ Michigan Central, Detroit. We manage enrollment, scheduling, and event coordination through an integrated web platform.

🤖 Clawd Agent Framework

The Python-based autonomous AI agent infrastructure that coordinates operational tasks across the entire enterprise. COO, CTO, Admin, Financial, and Apple Ecosystem agents all run on Clawd.

What’s Next

We’ll be publishing technical deep dives, sprint retrospectives, and architecture overviews on a regular cadence. Stay tuned.
