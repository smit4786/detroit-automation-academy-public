# Important Update: Student Orientation & Program Launch

To our valued students, parents, and partners,

We are writing to announce that the **Student Orientation** previously scheduled for today, March 5, 2026, has been **postponed**.

### Why the Postponement?
At Detroit Automation Academy, we are committed to providing a world-class, high-fidelity instructional experience. We are currently **improving our offering** by integrating new advanced robotics modules and enhancing our local-first digital infrastructure. 

To ensure that our first cohort has access to the absolute best tools and curriculum from Day 1, we have decided to push back the orientation and program start dates.

### What Happens Next?
- **New Dates:** We will announce the rescheduled orientation and program start dates within the next 14 days.
- **Stay Informed:** We encourage everyone to follow this blog for real-time updates.
- **Newsletter Invitation:** We will be launching an exclusive **DAA Insider Newsletter** next week. If you have already applied, you will receive an automated invitation to join.

### New Enrollment Welcome
If you are a new applicant, please look out for a **Welcome Email** arriving in your inbox shortly. This email will contain a detailed program overview, contact information for our leads, and your next steps while we finalize the new schedule.

Thank you for your patience and enthusiasm as we build the future of automation in Detroit.

Sincerely,

**Chief Marketing Officer (@CMO)**
Detroit Automation Academy
