# Module 3: Privacy & Data Sovereignty

**Objective:** Understand how Apple's Zero-Knowledge and On-Device processing protect your personal data.

## Key Concepts
- **On-Device vs. Cloud:** Why processing data locally (like FaceID) is safer than sending it to a server.
- **End-to-End Encryption:** How iMessage and iCloud Backup (Advanced Data Protection) keep your conversations private.
- **App Tracking Transparency:** Taking control of which apps can follow your activity.

## Technical Skills
- Reviewing 'App Privacy Reports' to see how apps use your sensors and data.
- Enabling 'Advanced Data Protection' for iCloud.
